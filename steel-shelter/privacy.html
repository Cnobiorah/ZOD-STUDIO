What we collect

When you submit the Request a Quote form, we collect the information you provide: your name, phone number, location, structure preferences, dimensions, and any photos you choose to upload.

How we use it

  • To respond to your quote request and arrange a site visit
  • To prepare a design and pricing proposal for your project
  • To contact you about your specific enquiry only

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We don't use your photos for anything beyond your own project unless you separately agree (for example, to feature a completed project in our portfolio)

Storage

Form submissions are stored securely and kept only as long as needed to process your request and, where a project goes ahead, for our project records.
